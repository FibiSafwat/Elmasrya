    Al Masrya is leading the development of a state-of-the-art hospital facility designed to serve the community with cutting-edge medical technology and patient-centered care. The facility will include specialized departments, advanced surgical suites, private patient rooms, and spacious recovery areas to enhance patient comfort and care. With an emphasis on safety and efficiency, the hospital is designed to streamline operations and offer a welcoming, healing environment for patients and staff alike. Expected to open [1 jan 2021], this hospital will be a beacon of healthcare excellence in the region.
